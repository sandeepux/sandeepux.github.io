About

Interaction design
as a craft

I'm a Full-stack Principal Designer based in Bangalore — working across the complete design discipline from user research and interaction design through to design systems and AI-integrated team practice. 15+ years designing enterprise B2B products across security, cloud, and endpoint management.

I tend to take on the hardest problems: the ones where the domain is genuinely complex, where users are experts under pressure, and where getting the interaction wrong costs real money or real time. I've done on-site contextual inquiry with healthcare systems, government transportation departments, and Fortune 500 enterprises.

I also think seriously about AI in design practice — not as a gimmick, but as a genuine shift in how design teams work. I've facilitated workshops on Tree of Thoughts prompting and Figma Make adoption, helping teams move from skepticism to fluency.
